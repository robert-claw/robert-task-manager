## LinkedIn Strategy (Primary Platform)

### TOFU: Awareness Posts
**Goal:** Get founders to know we exist and remember us

**Content Types:**
1. **Hot Takes** (1-2x/week)
   - Controversial opinions on AI development
   - "Why 2 weeks beats 6 months" arguments
   - Startup speed vs. perfection debates
   - Links to: MOFU educational posts or blog articles

2. **Founder Journey** (1x/week)
   - Behind-the-scenes of running Dandelion Labs
   - Lessons learned from client projects
   - Swiss tech scene observations
   - Links to: MOFU case studies

3. **Industry Commentary** (1x/week)
   - Reactions to AI news from founder perspective
   - VC trends and what they mean for startups
   - Technical developments demystified
   - Links to: MOFU deep-dives or blog posts

**Format:**
- Hook in first line (curiosity/controversy)
- 3-5 short paragraphs
- No fluff, direct language
- End with engagement question
- Subtle link to deeper content

**Success Metrics:**
- Impressions > 2K per post
- Comments > 10
- Profile visits > 50
- Followers +10-20/post

---

### MOFU: Consideration Posts
**Goal:** Prove expertise and get founders to engage deeper

**Content Types:**
1. **Educational Frameworks** (alternate weeks)
   - "How to scope an AI MVP in 1 hour"
   - "5 questions VCs ask about your AI product"
   - "The AI agency evaluation checklist"
   - Links to: Lead magnets (PDF download) OR case studies

2. **Case Study Teasers** (alternate weeks)
   - "How we built [feature] in 8 days"
   - Results-focused: "From 0 to 60% support automation"
   - Anonymized client wins with real numbers
   - Links to: Full case study on website OR discovery call

3. **Technical Credibility** (monthly)
   - RAG architecture decisions explained
   - LLM cost optimization tactics
   - When to use vs. not use AI
   - Links to: Blog deep-dive OR newsletter signup

**Format:**
- Problem â†’ Insight â†’ Framework/Solution
- Bullet points for readability
- Specific numbers, no vague claims
- CTA: Download template / Read full story / Join newsletter

**Success Metrics:**
- Engagement rate > 5%
- Click-through to website > 20 clicks
- Lead magnet downloads > 5/post
- Email signups > 3/post

---

### BOFU: Conversion Posts
**Goal:** Drive discovery call bookings

**Content Types:**
1. **Social Proof** (2x/month)
   - Client testimonials with results
   - "11 AI products shipped" milestone updates
   - Before/after comparisons
   - Direct CTA: Book discovery call

2. **Objection Handling** (1x/month)
   - "Why we only work with startups"
   - "What we say no to (and why that's good for you)"
   - "Fixed price vs. time & materials"
   - Direct CTA: "See if we're a fit" â†’ calendar link

3. **Time-Sensitive Offers** (quarterly)
   - "2 spots open in March"
   - "Book before [date] for [benefit]"
   - Limited availability messaging
   - Direct CTA: Calendar link

**Format:**
- Social proof upfront
- Clear value proposition
- Address fear/hesitation
- Strong, direct CTA
- No apologetic language

**Success Metrics:**
- Discovery calls booked > 2/post
- Calendar link clicks > 30
- DM inquiries > 3
