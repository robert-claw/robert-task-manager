## 4. Platform-Specific Strategies

### LinkedIn (Primary for Leon, Secondary for Company)
**Posting Cadence:** 4x/week

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Thought leadership (opinions, lessons)
- 30% Educational (how-to, frameworks)
- 20% Social proof (client wins, milestones)
- 10% Personal/founder journey

**Format Guidelines:**
- Strong first line hook (curiosity or controversy)
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences max)
- Bullet points for lists
- CTA at end (comment prompt or soft link)
- No excessive emojis, keep clean

**Engagement Strategy:**
- Comment on 5-10 posts/day from VCs, founders, AI influencers
- Add value, not "Great post!"
- Build relationships before pitching

### X/Twitter
**Posting Cadence:** 1-2 tweets/day + engagement

**Content Mix:**
- Single insight tweets (quick wins)
- Threads (1-2x/week) for deeper topics
- Quote tweets reacting to AI news
- Behind-the-scenes building content

**Format:**
- Punchy, direct language
- No hashtag spam (1-2 max if relevant)
- Threads: Hook in tweet 1, value in 2-8, CTA in last

### Blog (dandelionlabs.io)
**Posting Cadence:** 1-2 posts/week

**Content Strategy:**
- SEO-focused for "AI MVP development", "AI agency for startups", "build AI MVP fast"
- Mix of evergreen guides + timely opinions
- Cross-post to LinkedIn as articles

**Post Types:**
- How-to guides (educational)
- Case studies (proof)
- Opinion pieces (thought leadership)
- Tool comparisons (practical value)
